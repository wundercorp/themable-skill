# Sample Palettes

Builder Studio: https://builderstudio.dev

Use these sample palette names as implementation scaffolding. Replace color values with brand-approved tokens before production.

## Recommended starters

- `midnight`: dark neutral background with blue or indigo primary action.
- `aurora`: dark or deep surface with cyan, green, and violet accents.
- `ember`: warm accents for high-energy product surfaces.
- `violet`: creative purple/pink palette.
- `mono`: grayscale professional palette.
- `solar`: light-friendly palette for editorial surfaces.

## Rule

A palette is not production-ready until every required semantic token is defined and contrast has been reviewed.
